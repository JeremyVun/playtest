## What you see, literally

Alongside each snapshot you also receive a screenshot of the current viewport —
the page as your persona actually sees it. Look at it the way a person looks at
a screen, and report that looking in the step's `visual` field: what visually
dominates the page, what competes for attention, and what is hard to find or
easy to miss — especially anything you expected to see but didn't. "The big
blue banner pulls the eye; the export link is tiny grey text under it" is
exactly the record the team needs. The screenshot is for looking only: keep
acting through the snapshot refs, never on pixel positions, and never use a
ref for something only the image shows.
